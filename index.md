### Short Bio
I am currently a PhD candidate in Institute of Computing Technology (Third year at 2021), Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests primarily focus on the area of PIM, especially RRAM-based NN accelerator. I'm also interested in AutoML, with an emphasis on leveraging it to design energy efficient NN accelerator.
